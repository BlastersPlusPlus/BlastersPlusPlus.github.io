HP
-85
STR

Skill: Boosts damage to Oni-type foes by 15% for all allies.
Much like the Hammer of Despair, this equip had been left a bit behind compared to the others in its line.

Water Dragonblade

STR
320 → 370
Skill: Increases the power of Soultimate Moves by 50%.
Water Dragonblade always had quite a good skill, but an underwhelming STR stat. Now it's a quite good no-drawback equip.

Oni Warlord Sword

STR
340 → 350
DEF
0 → 30
Skill: 20% Atk 150% Def 40% MM boost 1 time when below 25% HP. →
Updated to match Master Nyada's
buffed skill.
As was the case with Master Nyada himself, the skill only triggering once for 20 seconds made this sword rather middling, something that was only worsened by its mediocre stats. A bit more STR and some bulk to pair with the updated skill take care of that.

Malicious Manacle

STR
230 → 280
DEF
80 → 130
Skill: Ignore the effects of foe’s guard. →
Cursed Skin
Guard break is a bad skill. Cursed Skin, on the other hand, has some cool uses and fits the item a lot more. Buffed stats on top of that.

Mayhem Manacle

STR
260 → 280
DEF
80 → 130
Skill: Strength & Defense up when in danger. →
15% STR & 105% DEF up when below 50% HP.
A defense increase at below 25% HP is just not great. Increasing the HP threshold at which it triggers and the effect itself should make it so that this item (and skill) become significantly more relevant. Besides that, its stats were also bad.

Demon Bracer

STR
275 → 295
SPR
-40

Skill: Criticals deal 75% more damage.
This equipment was already quite good despite the low STR (crits are just really strong), but an extra 30 in its overall stats makes it match the strongest of its line.

80 DEF isn't enough for a STR stat below 300 —a little bump to that should help a bit.

Wand of Fortune

Finally a suitable mage equip for skills like Dragon Lord's

Triple Fever Wand

STR
0 → 145
SPR
365
Skill: Increases damage of Fire Element attacks by 25%.
Slimamander
and Ogralus
go hand in hand in many ways —in particular, both their boss souls and playable forms lean strongly towards mixed sets. Wanting to improve options for mixed attackers in B++, we decided to also push that in their equip lines, making them parallels of each other. In particular, much like the Oni Smasher (Sea)
has some SPR, this equip now has some STR as well.

Frost Shield

HP
105
DEF
200 → 280
Skill: Reduces Ice Element damage. →
Reduces Ice Element damage by 50%.
There were many equipment items that reduce attribute damage (4 for each attribute!), all by 30% each. This is just... not great. All these skills have been buffed to reduce damage by 50%. They also generally had quite bad stats, which we've taken care of.

Lava Hogbucket

DEF
310 → 385
Skill: ↑ the damage of counters and reflects by 30%. →
Mirror body
This equipment's skill description was the same as Mirror Body's, but this description is separate from the skill itself. Internally, the skill is Bladed Body, which was changed along with Cutta-nah
. The equip has been fixed to use the correct skill and, on top of that, got some nice stat changes.

Antigravity Shield

DEF
340 → 380

All allies' speed up by 8%
You're running so fast it's like you don't feel the effects of gravity! Now this item has a reason to exist alongside its rank 6 counterpart
:)

Quantum Shield

DEF
360 → 380
Skill: Drops incoming damage by 20% and gives super armor.
This item's skill was buffed along with Sir Nyansalot's
, but its description didn't reflect that... until now.

Make-Up Bag

HP
105 → 205
STR
60 → 90
DEF
150 → 250
Skill: Reduces Earth Element damage. →
Increases area of effect for moves by 35%.
There are far too many Equipment items that reduce attribute damage —four for each attribute— and most of them are both weak and repetitive. For many, given their design and description, it makes too much sense not to have a different skill, but fortunately that wasn't the case for this one. We decided on a skill that wasn't present on Equipment previously and that would work quite well with the support Yo-kai this item is geared towards.

HP

DEF

Defense up by 140% when HP is full.

Much like other attribute resistance equips, we wanted to give it some other skill that would suit the item but be less repetitive. Channeling the power of the earth sounds like something that could totally be used to raise your team's stats, so we went with that.

How many times am I gonna have to explain that too many equips that reduce elemental damage are repetitive... Anyways, instead of quite literally warding off the dead, this item makes your team not go to the realm of the dead (at least not quite so fast).

Living World Cloth

HP
500
DEF
80 → 90
Skill: Attack up by 20% when HP is full. →
SPR up by 5% for all allies.
A skill like the one this item previously had on a defensive equip is honestly baffling. You deal way too little damage for the increase to be meaningful, and being at full HP with such a large stat becomes much harder. We opted for a support skill instead, letting defensive Yo-kai still offensively contribute, even if just a little bit.

SPR
61 → 100

I am SICK and TIRED of equip descriptions LYING to your face. No, a 30% damage reduction from water damage is not protecting you from all water damage. It's not a good skill either, so we fixed both issues.
