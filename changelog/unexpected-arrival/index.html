Balance Changes

  • Double Slice's damage has been increased to 33 and the crit rate to 50%.
  • One-Two Punch's damage has been increased to 44.
  • That All You Got? and Hit and Run now last until death.
  • Cicada Soul's effect duration has been reduced to 4 seconds.
  • Troubled Waters is now a holdable moves.
  • Adjusted the multi boost inspirit from Cat Cast Call.

Equipment Reworks

Changed the stats or skills on over 80 equipment items. Check out our Equipment Reworks page for more details and an explanation behind each change.
